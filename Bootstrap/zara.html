ZARA ORIGINS

My Purchases

Zara was started by Amancio Ortega in 1975. His first shop was in central A Coruña, in Galicia, Spain, where the company is still based. He initially called it Zorba after the classic 1964 film Zorba the Greek, but after learning there was a bar with the same name two blocks away, he rearranged the letters to read "Zara".
